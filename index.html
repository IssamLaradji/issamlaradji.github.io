About Dr. Laradji

Dr. Laradji is a Research Scientist at ServiceNow and Adjunct Professor at the University of British Columbia (UBC), focusing on machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing.

Specializing in large-scale optimization, few-shot learning, and self-supervised learning approaches with 50+ publications in top-tier conferences including NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, and CVPR.

He completed his PhD at the University of British Columbia and postdoctoral fellowship at McGill University.

Currently leading AI research initiatives for enterprise applications while supervising graduate students and teaching advanced ML courses.
