Issam Laradji is a research scientist at ServiceNow Research, where he works on reducing the amount of 
labeling needed to train machine learning models. He did his postdoc at McGill’s Graphics Lab 
and earned his PhD from the University of British Columbia’s Machine Learning Lab. 
His interests include natural language processing, computer vision (2D and 3D), and optimization. 
Issam also runs the Vancouver AI Academy, a community focused on AI learning and development.
