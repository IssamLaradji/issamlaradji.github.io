Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram

Description

The Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram (LBRP) is one of the most fundamental and important rituals in Western ceremonial magic. It serves to cleanse your space of negative energies, establish a sacred circle, and align yourself with the four elements and divine forces. This powerful protective ritual has been used by practitioners for over a century to prepare sacred space for magical work.

Purpose & Benefits

The Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram serves several important functions:

Cleanses your space of negative or unwanted energies
Creates a protective circle for magical workings
Balances the four elemental energies within and around you
Enhances focus and mental clarity for spiritual work
Opens channels for divine communication
Strengthens your aura and personal boundaries

This ritual is recommended for daily practice and is especially useful:

  • Before and after any magical or spiritual work
  • When entering a new or unfamiliar space
  • During times of spiritual confusion or psychic attack
  • As preparation for meditation or divination

Materials Needed

Gather these items before beginning

Ritual dagger (athame) or index finger
Optional: White candle
Optional: Incense (frankincense recommended)
Comfortable clothing (preferably clean and dedicated to ritual work)
Quiet space with enough room to move in a circle
