Dr. Issam Laradji

Senior Staff Research Scientist ¬∑ Adjunct Professor

Issam Laradji is a Senior Staff Research Scientist at ServiceNow and an Adjunct Professor at the University of British Columbia. His work focuses on building frontier, human-centered, state-of-the-art yet efficient AI models that help people achieve their goals in the best possible way. He has also developed reliable and reproducible benchmarks to ensure that AI systems are scalable, follow the right protocols, and can be deployed safely and reliably. He holds a postdoctoral fellowship from McGill University and a PhD and MSc from the University of British Columbia, with expertise in natural language processing, computer vision, and optimization.

Issam regularly runs workshops, crash courses, and lectures to help people learn about AI and apply it to real-world goals. He is deeply passionate about making AI more accessible to everyone and leads an inclusive, thriving community grounded in collaboration and shared learning. His vision is to create an environment where committed teams can connect and work together on ambitious projects that spark curiosity, drive innovation, and expand what humans can achieve.
